Historical Bridges in the Philippines

Puente de Reina Isabel II

The bridge was constructed under the term of Gobernador Don Jose Maria de la O in March 15, 1852. It was finished in July 16, 1853 and was dedicated to Queen Isabel II of Spain.

San Juanico Bridge

The San Juanico Bridge that links the islands of Samar and Leyte has held the title of the longest bridge in the Philippines.

Puente de Malagonlong
